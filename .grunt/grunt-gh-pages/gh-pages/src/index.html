Template Features

This template was intentionally given the appearance of depth.
The layers, from front to back, are as follows:

  • Sticky-Navigation (The dropdown at the top)
  • The footer ground and static elements (mountains, large tree, icons, etc)
  • The animated trees (triggered by your scroll location)
  • The page content
  • The Clouds
  • The Sky

This template is also highly responsive.
You'll notice that when you shrink your window, stuff happens!

  • Shrink your browser window
  • See how elements change size?
  • If you get small enough, these lists will actually stack on top of each other!
  • Even the menu changes size
  • Everything needs to fit, even on small phones
